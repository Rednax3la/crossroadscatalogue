My Sexuality & Resisting Pressure

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: September 13, 2026
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To provide biblical understanding of sexuality, teach strategies for resisting peer pressure, and affirm God's design for purity and healthy relationships.
1. Opening Activity (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "Boundary Lines"
Physical
Emotional
Social
Digital
My Personal Boundaries

Teens reflect on and share:

"One boundary that's important to me is..."
"I feel pressured when..."
"My faith influences my boundaries by..."

Discussion: "God gives us boundaries for our protection and flourishing. Today we'll explore healthy boundaries in relationships."

2. God's Design for Sexuality (15 min)
15 min
A. Biblical View of Sexuality

God's design for relationships:

  • Created male and female in God's image (Genesis 1:27)
  • Sexuality as a sacred gift within marriage
  • Our bodies as temples of the Holy Spirit (1 Cor 6:19)
  • Honoring God with our bodies
"Do you not know that your bodies are temples of the Holy Spirit?" 1 Corinthians 6:19
B. Understanding Pressures

Common sources of pressure:

Peer Pressure
Media Influence
Digital/Social Media
Romantic Relationships
"Do not conform to the pattern of this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind." Romans 12:2
C. The Value of Purity

Why abstinence matters:

  • Protects emotional and physical health
  • Honors God's design
  • Builds trust in relationships
  • Prepares for future marriage
"It is God's will that you should be sanctified: that you should avoid sexual immorality." 1 Thessalonians 4:3
3. Resisting Pressure (20 min)
20 min
Activity: "Pressure Response Practice"
Simple "No"
Walk Away
Change Subject
Strength in Numbers
Faith Commitment
Role-Play Scenarios
"Everyone's dating - why don't you have a boyfriend/girlfriend?"
"Just send one picture - it's no big deal"
"We've been together for months - if you loved me, you would..."
"It's just kissing/making out - it doesn't mean anything"

Affirmation of Purity

"I choose to honor God with my body and relationships. My worth comes from Christ, not from others' opinions."

4. Commitment & Closing (10 min)
10 min
Purity Commitment

My Commitment to Purity

Today, I commit to honoring God with my body, mind, and relationships. I will:

  • Respect the boundaries God has given me
  • Resist pressure that goes against my values
  • Seek accountability in my relationships
  • View myself as God's beloved child
"Flee from sexual immorality. All other sins a person commits are outside the body, but whoever sins sexually, sins against their own body." 1 Corinthians 6:18

Closing Prayer: "Heavenly Father, thank you for creating us as sexual beings in your image. Give us wisdom to navigate relationships with integrity. Strengthen us to resist pressure and stand firm in our commitment to purity. Help us to see ourselves and others through your eyes of love and grace. May our relationships honor you and reflect your design. Amen."
