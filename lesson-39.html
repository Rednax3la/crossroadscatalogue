Duration: 50 minutes
Date: September 28, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To help teens understand biblical maturity, recognize characteristics of spiritual growth, and develop practical habits for becoming mature followers of Christ.

2. Biblical Maturity (15 min)
15 min
A. What is Spiritual Maturity?

Beyond physical age to Christlike character:

  • Growing in knowledge and application of God's Word
  • Developing Christlike character (Galatians 5:22-23)
  • Taking responsibility for faith and actions
  • Serving others selflessly
"Until we all reach unity in the faith and in the knowledge of the Son of God and become mature, attaining to the whole measure of the fullness of Christ." Ephesians 4:13
B. Characteristics of Maturity

Signs of growing spiritual maturity:

Responsibility
Self-Control
Service
Wisdom
"When I was a child, I talked like a child, I thought like a child, I reasoned like a child. When I became a man, I put the ways of childhood behind me." 1 Corinthians 13:11

4. Commitment to Growth (10 min)
10 min
Maturity Commitment

My Growth Commitment

I commit to pursuing spiritual maturity through:

  • Daily engagement with God's Word
  • Intentional development of Christlike character
  • Faithful service to others
  • Accountability in my spiritual journey
  • Patience in the growth process
"But grow in the grace and knowledge of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. To him be glory both now and forever! Amen." 2 Peter 3:18

Closing Prayer: "Heavenly Father, thank you for calling us to grow in Christlikeness. Give us the desire and discipline to pursue spiritual maturity. Help us to be patient with ourselves and others in the growth process. May we become mature followers who reflect your love and grace to the world. Amen."
