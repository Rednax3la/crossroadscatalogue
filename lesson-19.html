Privileges Responsibilities

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: May 11, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Mother's Day Special
Objective: To help teens understand the relationship between privileges and responsibilities, with special focus on appreciating mothers and mother figures, recognizing their sacrifices, and fulfilling our responsibilities toward them.
1. Opening Activity (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "Privilege vs Responsibility"

Teens match privileges with corresponding responsibilities:

Privileges
Using phone
Having freedom
Receiving love
Getting an education
Responsibilities
Using wisely
Making good choices
Showing love back
Studying diligently

Discussion: "Every privilege comes with responsibility. Today we'll explore this balance, especially in relationship to our mothers."

2. Understanding Privileges & Responsibilities (15 min)
15 min
A. Biblical Foundations
  • Stewardship principle (Luke 12:48)
  • Honoring parents commandment (Exodus 20:12)
  • Jesus' example of responsibility
"From everyone who has been given much, much will be demanded; and from the one who has been entrusted with much, much more will be asked." Luke 12:48
B. Mother's Day Focus

Honoring Mothers

Recognizing the privilege of having a mother/mother figure and our responsibility to honor them

"Honor your father and mother - which is the first commandment with a promise - so that it may go well with you and that you may enjoy long life on the earth." Ephesians 6:2-3
C. The Mother's Privilege & Responsibility
  • Privilege of nurturing life
  • Responsibility of guidance and care
  • Sacrifices mothers make
  • Balancing love and discipline

20 min

Create a Mother's Day Card

4. Commitment (10 min)
10 min
My Responsibilities Commitment

30-Day Responsibility Challenge

Week 1: Household Help
Week 2: Communication
Week 3: Appreciation
Week 4: Honor Actions
"Listen, my son, to your father's instruction and do not forsake your mother's teaching. They are a garland to grace your head and a chain to adorn your neck." Proverbs 1:8-9
My Commitment to My Mother/Mother Figure:

I commit to showing appreciation by...

I will take responsibility for...

I will honor you by...

Closing Prayer: "Heavenly Father, thank you for the gift of mothers and mother figures. Help us to recognize the privileges we enjoy and embrace our responsibilities. Teach us to honor our mothers not just on Mother's Day, but every day. Give us wisdom to balance our privileges with responsibility, and hearts full of gratitude for those who care for us. Amen."

Facilitator Tips
  • Be sensitive to teens with complicated family situations
  • Emphasize appreciation for all mother figures (grandmothers, aunts, mentors)
  • Provide art supplies for card-making
  • Share your own experiences with privilege and responsibility
  • Discuss how to honor parents when relationships are difficult
  • Follow up next week about their responsibility challenge
