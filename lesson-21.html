Who Am I? Identity in Christ

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: May 25, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To help teens discover their true identity in Christ, moving beyond societal labels to understand their inherent worth as children of God and their unique purpose in His kingdom.
1. Opening Activity (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "Labels & Identity"

Teens share labels they've been given or have given themselves:

What labels do others put on you?
What labels do you put on yourself?
How do these labels make you feel?

Transition: "Today we'll look beyond labels to discover our true identity in Christ."

Loved

Closing Prayer: "Heavenly Father, thank you for creating me with purpose and love. Help me to see myself as you see me - your beloved child. When I'm tempted to believe the world's labels, remind me of my true identity in Christ. Give me the courage to walk in the truth of who you say I am. Amen."

Facilitator Tips
  • Create a safe space for vulnerability
  • Share your own identity journey
  • Use actual mirrors as visual aids
  • Provide identity affirmation cards
  • Discuss social media's impact on identity
  • Follow up with teens struggling with identity
