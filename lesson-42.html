Dreams And Ambitions

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: October 19, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To help teens discover their God-given dreams and ambitions, teaching them how to set goals, overcome obstacles, and align their aspirations with God's purpose for their lives.
1. Opening Activity (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "Dream Big"

Teens complete these statements:

"In 10 years, I hope to..."
"My biggest dream is..."
"I want to make a difference in..."
"God has given me a passion for..."

Discussion: "God plants dreams in our hearts. Today we'll learn how to discover and pursue those God-given ambitions."

2. Discovering Your Dreams (15 min)
15 min
A. God-Given Dreams

Understanding the source of true dreams:

  • God plants desires in our hearts (Psalm 37:4)
  • Dreams that honor God and serve others
  • Aligning with our gifts and talents
  • Bringing glory to God, not ourselves
"Take delight in the LORD, and he will give you the desires of your heart." Psalm 37:4

C. Dream Killers

Common obstacles to overcome:

  • Fear of failure
  • Lack of confidence
  • Comparison to others
  • Impatience
  • Distractions
"For God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power and of love and of a sound mind." 2 Timothy 1:7

4. Dream Commitment (10 min)
10 min
Dream Commitment

My Dream Covenant

I commit to pursuing my God-given dreams by:

Seeking God's Guidance
Setting Regular Goals
Developing My Skills

"Commit to the LORD whatever you do, and he will establish your plans." Proverbs 16:3

Closing Prayer: "Heavenly Father, thank you for planting dreams in our hearts. Give us wisdom to recognize the dreams that come from you. Help us to be courageous in pursuing them, persistent in developing our skills, and patient in waiting for your timing. May our dreams bring glory to you and blessing to others. Amen."

Dream-Chasing Tips for Teens
  • Pray about your dreams regularly
  • Break big dreams into small, achievable steps
  • Find a mentor in your area of interest
  • Read biographies of people who achieved similar dreams
  • Don't be discouraged by setbacks - they're part of the journey
