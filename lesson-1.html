10 min

Section 1: Opening Icebreaker

Activity: “Spiritual Road Trip”
“If your faith journey was a road trip, what kind of vehicle would you be in and why?”
“Who would be your travel companions on this spiritual adventure?”
“What is one destination you hope to reach in your relationship with God this year?”

Just like a road trip, our faith journey has a starting point, destinations, and companions. Crossroads is designed to be the roadmap for this adventure.

15 min

Section 2: Understanding Crossroads

A. What is Crossroads?

A year-long spiritual journey for teens.

“Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight.”
Proverbs 3:5–6
B. Why a Journey Metaphor?

Faith is a lifelong adventure — not a single event. Along the way we need:

  • A roadmap
  • Companions
  • Tools
“Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labor: If either of them falls down, one can help the other up.”
Ecclesiastes 4:9–10
C. Our Guiding Principles

Discover · Grow · Impact

“…he who began a good work in you will carry it on to completion until the day of Christ Jesus.”
Philippians 1:6
20 min

Section 3: Interactive Activity

Activity: “Faith Journey Mapping”
A. Roadmap Creation

Draw where you started, spiritual milestones, your current location, and future destinations you hope to reach.

B. Crossroads Signposts

Mark important decisions, challenges you have overcome, and people who have influenced your journey.

Facilitator Note

Walk around the room, ask open-ended questions, and model vulnerability by sharing your own map first.

10 min

Section 4: Commitment & Closing

Activity: Group Commitment

Create a Crossroads Covenant together — each teen commits to:

  • Attending each session
  • Participating openly and honestly
  • Respecting others in the group
  • Bringing their Bible, notebook, and pen
Weekly Challenge

Share your faith journey map with one person this week.

Scripture
“Therefore, since we are surrounded by such a great cloud of witnesses, let us throw off everything that hinders and the sin that so easily entangles. And let us run with perseverance the race marked out for us, fixing our eyes on Jesus, the pioneer and perfecter of faith.”
Hebrews 12:1–2
Closing Prayer

“Lord, as we begin this Crossroads journey together, open our hearts to your guidance. Give us courage to navigate the twists and turns of faith, and help us grow closer to you each step of the way. Amen.”

Facilitator Tips
  • Share your own personal crossroads story to open up discussion
  • Bring a physical road map as a visual aid
  • Play “Where the Road Leads” softly during the mapping activity
  • Provide art supplies — coloured pens, stickers, markers
