Media & Technology

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: August 31, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To help teens navigate the digital world with wisdom, discernment, and intentionality, understanding both the benefits and challenges of media and technology from a Christian perspective.
1. Digital Life Check-in (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "Screen Time Reflection"
Low Moderate High Excessive

Teens position the slider to represent their average daily screen time:

"The app I use most is..."
"I feel ____ about my tech habits"
"One positive way I've used tech recently..."

Discussion: "Technology is a powerful tool - today we'll explore how to use it wisely as Christians."

2. Digital Discipleship (15 min)
15 min
A. Biblical Perspective on Technology

Scriptural principles for technology use:

  • All things permissible but not beneficial (1 Cor 10:23)
  • Setting our minds on higher things (Col 3:2)
  • Being wise as serpents (Matt 10:16)
  • Everything should build up (1 Cor 14:26)
"I have the right to do anything... but not everything is beneficial." 1 Corinthians 10:23
B. Digital Dangers

Challenges to be aware of:

  • Addiction and time consumption
  • Comparison and envy
  • Exposure to harmful content
  • Cyberbullying and online predators
  • Distraction from real relationships
"Be alert and of sober mind. Your enemy the devil prowls around like a roaring lion..." 1 Peter 5:8
C. Digital Opportunities

Positive ways to use technology:

  • Accessing biblical resources
  • Connecting with Christian communities
  • Sharing faith and encouragement
  • Learning and skill development
  • Creativity and expression
"And whatever you do, whether in word or deed, do it all in the name of the Lord Jesus..." Colossians 3:17
3. Digital Discernment Lab (20 min)
20 min
Activity: "App Evaluation"
Evaluation Criteria:
How much time does it consume?
How does it affect my emotions?
Does it align with Christian values?
How does it impact my relationships?

Digital Discernment Challenge

Evaluate your top 3 apps using these criteria this week

4. Creating Balance (10 min)
10 min
Healthy Tech Habits

Spiritual First

Bible before phone in the morning

Time Limits

Use app timers and screen time tracking

Tech-Free Zones

No devices at meals or in bedrooms

Human Connection

Prioritize face-to-face conversations

"So whether you eat or drink or whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God." 1 Corinthians 10:31

Closing Prayer: "Lord, thank you for the gift of technology. Help us to use it wisely and for your glory. Give us discernment to recognize harmful influences and courage to make healthy choices. May our digital lives reflect your love and truth. Guide us to balance our online and offline worlds in ways that honor you. Amen."
