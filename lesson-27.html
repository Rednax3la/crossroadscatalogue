Bible Models & Role Models

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: July 6, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To help teens identify and learn from positive role models in the Bible and in their lives, while understanding how to become godly examples for others.
1. Opening Activity (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "My Hero Spotlight"

Teens share about someone they admire:

"A person I look up to because..."
"Someone whose character inspires me..."
"A quality I admire in others..."
"How I want to influence others..."

Discussion: "We all need role models to guide and inspire us. Today we'll explore biblical heroes and learn what makes a godly role model."

2. Biblical Role Models (15 min)
15 min
A. Why Role Models Matter

The importance of godly examples:

  • They show us what's possible
  • Provide guidance and inspiration
  • Help us avoid mistakes through their examples
  • Demonstrate how to live out faith practically
"Remember your leaders, who spoke the word of God to you. Consider the outcome of their way of life and imitate their faith." Hebrews 13:7

3. Role Model Exploration (20 min)
20 min
Activity: "Role Model Profile"
Bible Model

Select a Bible character to learn about their life and qualities

Modern Role Model

Select a role model from your life to reflect on their qualities

Qualities I Admire
Faith
Kindness
Wisdom
Courage
Generosity
Communication
Mentor Match
Spiritual Mentor: Who guides your faith journey?
Knowledge Mentor: Who teaches you skills and knowledge?
Character Mentor: Who models integrity and character?
4. Becoming a Role Model (10 min)
10 min
The Ripple Effect

Role Model Challenge

Identify 3 role model qualities to develop
Thank a role model in your life
Mentor someone younger
Study a Bible character monthly
"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity." 1 Timothy 4:12

Closing Prayer: "God, thank you for the examples of faith you've placed in Scripture and in our lives. Help us to learn from these role models and to develop Christlike character. Give us wisdom to choose good influences and courage to become positive role models for others. May our lives point others to you. Amen."

Role Model Tips
  • Study the lives of biblical characters regularly
  • Seek out mentors in different areas of life
  • Be intentional about who you allow to influence you
  • Remember that you're always modeling behavior to someone
  • Develop self-awareness about your influence on others
