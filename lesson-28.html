Friends Family & Faith

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: July 13, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To help teens navigate relationships with friends and family through the lens of faith, establishing healthy boundaries while building Christ-centered connections.
1. Opening Activity (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "Relationship Map"

Teens reflect on their important relationships:

"One person who supports my faith..."
"A family member I connect with..."
"A friend who challenges me positively..."
"Someone I need to set better boundaries with..."

Discussion: "Our relationships deeply impact our faith journey. Today we'll learn how to build Christ-centered connections with friends and family."

15 min

Characteristics of faith-building friendships:

Unconditional Love
"As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another." Proverbs 27:17
C. Family Relationships

Honoring family while growing in faith:

  • Honor your parents (Exodus 20:12)
  • Communicate with respect
  • Seek understanding
  • Show grace in conflicts
  • Be a peacemaker
"Children, obey your parents in the Lord, for this is right." Ephesians 6:1

20 min

4. Faith-Centered Commitment (10 min)
10 min
Relationship Renewal

7-Day Relationship Challenge

Day 1: Pray for a family member
Day 2: Encourage a friend
Day 3: Set a healthy boundary
Day 4: Share your faith
Day 5: Resolve a conflict
Day 6: Quality family time
Day 7: Faith conversation
Pray for relationships daily
Apply Scripture to relationships
Build faith-based friendships
Show Christ's love to family
"Above all, love each other deeply, because love covers over a multitude of sins." 1 Peter 4:8

Closing Prayer: "Lord, thank you for the gift of relationships. Help us to put you at the center of all our connections. Give us wisdom to build healthy friendships, grace to honor our families, and courage to set godly boundaries. May our relationships reflect your love and draw others to you. Amen."

Relationship Tips
  • Schedule regular family meals or activities
  • Find faith-based activities to do with friends
  • Set aside dedicated device-free time for relationships
  • Practice active listening without distractions
  • Express appreciation regularly
