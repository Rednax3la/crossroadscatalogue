Crossroads 2025 Launch

Duration: 75 minutes
Date: February 2, 2025
Participants: Teens & Parents
Objective: To officially launch the Crossroads 2025 program by building excitement, establishing community, and aligning parents and teens around the program's vision for spiritual growth through rites of passage experiences.
1. Opening Connection (15 min)
15 min
Activity: "Faith Journey Interviews"

Teens and parents pair up (not in same families) for quick interviews:

Questions for Teens

"What are you most excited about for Crossroads?"
"What spiritual skill do you want to develop this year?"

Questions for Parents

"What was a significant faith moment in your teen years?"
"How do you hope Crossroads will impact your teen?"

Facilitator Note: After 5 minutes, have pairs share highlights with another pair. Conclude by emphasizing the intergenerational faith journey.

2. Crossroads Vision (20 min)
20 min
A. Why Rites of Passage?

In a world without clear transitions, Crossroads provides:

  • Intentional spiritual milestones
  • Community recognition of growth
  • Tools for faith ownership
  • Bridge between childhood and mature faith
"When I was a child, I talked like a child... When I became a man, I put the ways of childhood behind me." 1 Corinthians 13:11
B. The Crossroads Journey

Overview of the year's roadmap:

  • Road 1: Foundations - Identity in Christ
  • Road 2: My Story, God's Story - Faith narrative
  • Road 3: Walking the Talk - Spiritual disciplines
  • Road 4: Equipped for Journey - Life skills
  • Road 5: CPR - Relationships & decisions
"Trust in the LORD with all your heart... and he will make your paths straight." Proverbs 3:5-6
C. Partnership Roles

How each group contributes:

Teens Commit To:

  • Active participation
  • Openness to growth
  • Respectful engagement

Parents Commit To:

  • Supporting the process
  • Follow-up conversations
  • Prayer covering
3. Family Faith Mapping (25 min)
25 min
Activity: "Generational Roadmap"

Families create visual representations of their faith journeys:

Family Activity

Step 1: Individual Starting Points

Each member marks their spiritual birthplace on the timeline

Step 2: Significant Milestones

Add key faith moments for each person (baptism, mission trip, etc.)

Step 3: Crossroads Intersection

Mark where family journeys converge for this program

Step 4: Future Destinations

Envision where each person hopes to grow spiritually this year

Facilitator Note: Provide large poster paper, markers, and stickers. Encourage families to display their maps around the room. Play background music like "The Stand" by Hillsong.

4. Dedication & Commissioning (15 min)
15 min
Crossroads Covenant

Unified commitment to the Crossroads journey:

We Commit To:

As Teens:

  • Engaging fully in each session
  • Respecting facilitators and peers
  • Being open to God's transformation

As Parents:

  • Supporting the Crossroads process
  • Creating space for spiritual conversations
  • Praying for the program and participants
Family Signature
Prayer Commitment
Journey Pledge
"But as for me and my household, we will serve the LORD." Joshua 24:15

Commissioning Prayer: "Lord of the Journey, we dedicate this Crossroads program to you. Bless these teens as they embark on this rite of passage. Strengthen parents as guides and supporters. Unite us as a community seeking you. May this year be transformative as we journey together toward spiritual maturity. Amen."

Bonus Tips for Facilitators
  • Create a photo booth with Crossroads-themed props
  • Provide a special launch dessert for fellowship time
  • Invite previous Crossroads graduates to share testimonies
  • Give each family a "Journey Journal" to record insights
  • Set up a prayer wall for written commitments
