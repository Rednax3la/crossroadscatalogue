iContribute Making a Difference

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: July 27, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To inspire teens to discover their unique gifts and use them to make meaningful contributions to their community, church, and world, living out their faith through service.
1. Opening Activity (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "My Contribution Seed"

Teens reflect on how they've contributed to others:

"One way I've helped someone recently..."
"A talent I can use to serve others..."
"A cause I care about..."
"How I want to make a difference..."

Discussion: "God created each of us with unique gifts to contribute to the world. Today we'll discover how you can make a meaningful difference."

15 min

4. Contribution Challenge (10 min)
10 min
iContribute Commitment

30-Day Contribution Challenge

Week 1: One church contribution
Week 2: One community contribution
Week 3: One school contribution
Week 4: One family contribution
"Do not neglect to do good and to share what you have, for such sacrifices are pleasing to God." Hebrews 13:16
Commit to regular service
Find a service buddy
Journal your impact

Closing Prayer: "Lord, thank you for creating us to make a difference in this world. Open our eyes to see opportunities to contribute. Give us courage to step out and use our gifts to serve others. May our contributions bring glory to you and make a lasting impact in our communities. Amen."

Contribution Tips
  • Start small - even little contributions matter
  • Match your service to your talents and passions
  • Make service a regular habit, not just occasional
  • Invite friends to serve with you
  • Reflect on how your contributions make a difference
