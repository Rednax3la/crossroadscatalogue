Self-Worth & Self-Esteem

Duration: 50 minutes
Date: June 1, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
Objective: To help teens understand their inherent worth as creations of God, develop healthy self-esteem based on biblical truth, and recognize the difference between worldly and godly measures of value.
1. Opening Activity (10 min)
10 min
Activity: "Value Auction"

Teens bid on items with imaginary money:

Popularity
Good Grades
Athletic Ability
Being Loved by God

Discussion: "How do we determine value? Today we'll explore what gives us true worth as individuals."

2. Biblical Self-Worth (15 min)
15 min
A. Our Intrinsic Value
  • Created in God's image (Genesis 1:27)
  • Unconditional worth, not based on performance
  • Worth that can't be earned or lost
"I praise you because I am fearfully and wonderfully made; your works are wonderful, I know that full well." Psalm 139:14
B. Healthy vs Unhealthy Self-Esteem
Low Self-Esteem
Healthy Self-Worth
Arrogance

Biblical self-worth avoids both self-hatred and self-worship

"Do not think of yourself more highly than you ought, but rather think of yourself with sober judgment." Romans 12:3
C. Sources of Worth
God's Love

Unchanging, unconditional

Performance

Conditional, unstable

Others' Approval

Unreliable, shifting

Appearance

Temporary, superficial

3. Identity Transformation (20 min)
20 min
Activity: "From Lies to Truth"

Common Lies

"I'm not good enough"
"I don't matter"
"I'm unlovable"
"I'm a failure"

Biblical Truths

"I am God's masterpiece"
"I have eternal value"
"I am deeply loved"
"I am being perfected"
Mirror of Truth
I am fearfully and wonderfully made (Psalm 139:14)
I am chosen and loved by God (1 Thessalonians 1:4)
I am God's masterpiece (Ephesians 2:10)
I am more than a conqueror (Romans 8:37)
I am a child of God (1 John 3:1)
Personal Affirmation
I am valuable because God says I am...
My worth comes from...
Today I will remember...
4. Worth Walk Challenge (10 min)
10 min
30-Day Worth Walk

Daily Affirmation Challenge

Week 1: God's Creation
Week 2: God's Love
Week 3: God's Purpose
Week 4: God's Power
"Are not two sparrows sold for a penny? Yet not one of them will fall to the ground outside your Father's care. So don't be afraid; you are worth more than many sparrows." Matthew 10:29, 31

Closing Prayer: "Heavenly Father, thank you for creating me with infinite worth. Help me to see myself as you see me - valuable, loved, and purposefully designed. When I'm tempted to doubt my worth, remind me of your unchanging love. Teach me to find my identity in you alone. Amen."

Facilitator Tips
  • Share your own struggles with self-worth
  • Provide small mirrors for affirmation exercises
  • Discuss social media's impact on self-image
  • Create a "Wall of Truth" with scripture affirmations
  • Address comparison and envy issues
  • Follow up with teens who seem to struggle
