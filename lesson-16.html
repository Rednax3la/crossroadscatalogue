Easter Sunday Resurrection Celebration

Duration: 60 minutes
Date: April 20, 2025
Age Group: 13-14 years
HE IS RISEN!
Objective: To celebrate the resurrection of Jesus Christ and explore its significance for our faith, helping teens understand how the resurrection changes everything and gives us hope for eternal life.

Facilitator Tips
  • Create a "Resurrection Wall" with notes of thanksgiving
  • Provide Easter treats: resurrection rolls, empty tomb cookies
  • Use bright decorations - white, gold, and purple fabrics
  • Invite teens to share personal resurrection stories
  • End with a celebratory song and dance
